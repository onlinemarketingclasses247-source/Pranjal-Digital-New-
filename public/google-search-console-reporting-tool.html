⚙️ How It Works – Simple 3‑Step Process

1. Export

Go to Google Search Console → Performance report → Export as Excel (.xlsx).

2. Upload

Drag & drop or click the box above. Your file stays 100% local.

3. Analyze

Instantly get top pages, queries, countries, devices, AI Q&A, and a professional PDF report.

The entire process takes less than 60 seconds. Our proprietary algorithms automatically detect columns (URLs, clicks, impressions, CTR, position) from any GSC export format. You don’t need to clean or rename data. After upload, the dashboard presents 10+ intelligence features including quick wins, brand vs non‑brand split, traffic dependency risk, and content gap signals. All insights update instantly when you apply filters. The tool is designed for SEO professionals, business owners, and agencies who need fast, actionable data without the complexity of spreadsheets.
